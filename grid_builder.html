Ghost blocks are just props, and won't be visible on the site.

Block dimensions may vary slightly because of the way Masonry rounds pixel values (see issue). This can cause items to stack in unexpected ways. For example, imagine that the top-leftmost block is invisibly taller than the block to its right. If you were to prepend a new block, it will appear in the lowest slot that is wide enough for it to fit into, and in some cases this is after the top-leftmost block. Results vary between Firefox and Chrome.

To fix this, throw .5px ghost blocks beneath blocks that you wish were taller. Finally, always test on both Firefox and Chrome.
